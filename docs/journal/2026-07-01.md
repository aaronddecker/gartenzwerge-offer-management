## Learnings

- The reload logout was a race: an aborted `/api/auth/me` rejected and the catch-all wiped the token. The fix is to distinguish a real 401 (`UnauthorizedError`) from transient/aborted errors.
- An SVG with `viewBox` + `width:100%` scales its text with the container. That helps mobile (12 month labels shrink to fit) but on wide desktop the labels dwarfed the headings. Capping the width keeps the mobile downscaling and stops the desktop upscaling.
- `width:100% + max-width + margin:auto` does not center — the auto margins are computed against the full width. Centering a capped block is more robust with a flex parent.
- A separate demo database plus a reproducible seeder gives clean, regenerable screenshots without polluting local dev data.
